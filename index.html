Deal Insights

Rule-based checks against common underwriting benchmarks — not AI/ML, just a set of if/then heuristics applied automatically so they're not missed.

Scenario Analysis

Stress-tests your base case against a downside (rent −10%, vacancy +5pp, maintenance +2pp, rate +1.0pp) and an upside (rent +5%, vacancy −2pp, rate −0.25pp), holding purchase price and financing structure constant.

Long-Term Projection

Projects equity buildup and cash flow over a hold period, with adjustable appreciation, rent growth, expense growth, and estimated selling costs at exit.
